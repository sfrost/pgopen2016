POSTGRESOPEN 2016

DALLAS, TX | SEP 13-16 2016

  • CHECK THE SCHEDULE

Join Us in Dallas!

PostgresOpen 2016 will be held on September 13 - September 16th in Dallas, at the Westin Galleria.
The conference delivers three days packed with tutorials and presentations about PostgreSQL and related technologies, one day of extended trainings, and the usual hallway and social track!

PostgreSQL Community

For over 20 years, PostgreSQL Community works to organize conferences with many technical and business talks by developers and key users globally.
In addition to PostgresOpen, PostgreSQL Europe will be holding a conference in the Fall of 2016 - 2016.pgconf.eu.
